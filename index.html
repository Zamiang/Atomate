Get and stay organized

Atomate helps you stay organized by giving you one place to find and update all the things you need for you day. Our priority is to make it easy for you to add stuff and find suff. We do this by infering what type of data you enter as you enter it, by alowing you to enter many different types of things and by importing your information from the apps you use every day. All your information is in one list-- accessible, filterable and searchable from your phone and your web browser.
